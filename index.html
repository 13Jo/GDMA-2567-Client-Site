Services

Our lessons are specifically designed to teach you to surf at your own pace in a relaxed environment to build further self-confidence so you can continue in the sport of surfing and feel confident in the water.

With each lesson we cover Ocean safety. This ensures that at the end of your experience you have knowledge to know where is a safe area to surf or swim for the future.

You will also receive Half price dinner voucher and complimentary drink at a local restaurant with each lesson.

About Us

As a mobile surf school we are dedicated to finding the best, fun and safest waves daily.

We have over 10 different locations at our fingertips, ready for the ultimate learn to surf experience.

Our beaches are the best in New Zealand, you will learn in waist deep water with easy to ride small waves.

We are the only Manu Bay surf school to have surf school permits to teach personal private lessons and group lessons in both Manu and Whale Bay.

Endless Summer Surf School offers programs to complete beginners, intermediate and advanced surfers of any age.
